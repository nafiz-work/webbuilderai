Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Swinging Hammer Home Repair, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and home address that you provide when requesting a quote or contacting us.

How We Use Your Information
